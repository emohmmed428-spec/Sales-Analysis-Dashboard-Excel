# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel Project)

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior using Excel.
The goal is to identify key business issues affecting profitability and provide actionable recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Despite strong sales performance, the company faces inconsistent profitability and occasional losses.
The objective is to identify the root causes and improve decision-making.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset includes:

* Sales
* Profit
* Discount
* Customer information
* Product categories (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies)

---

## 🔍 Analysis Process

### 1. Data Cleaning

* Removed duplicate records
* Validated dataset consistency
* Final dataset: 7,456 rows

---

### 2. Data Analysis

* Sales & Profit analysis by category
* Customer segmentation (Profitable / Neutral / Loss)
* Discount impact on profitability
* Product-level analysis (Root Cause)

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Technology is the highest-performing category in terms of sales and profit
* Office Supplies and Furniture contribute significantly to losses
* Approximately **18–19% of customers generate losses**
* High discount levels and repeated sales of low-margin products drive losses
* Losses are concentrated in specific products, not entire categories

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* KPIs:

  * Total Sales
  * Total Profit
  * Total Orders
  * Profit Margin

* Interactive filters:

  * Category
  * Region
  * Segment
  * Month

* Visualizations:

  * Sales by Category
  * Profit by Category
  * Sales by Month
  * Profit vs Discount
  * Top Customers
  * Loss-making Products

---

## 💡 Recommendations

1. Implement stricter control over discount strategies, especially for low-margin products
2. Focus on high-performing and high-margin products
3. Apply discounts strategically (seasonal campaigns instead of continuous discounts)
4. Investigate loss-making products and adjust pricing or positioning
